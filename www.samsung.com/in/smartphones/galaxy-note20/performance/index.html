This is a PC in your pocket with the power to do things unlike any PC

DYNAMIC AMOLED 2X

The most vivid and brightest in a smartphone

The Dynamic AMOLED 2X Infinity-O Display on Galaxy Note20 Ultra delivers 1500 nits for a colorful, glare-free view, even in bright daylight.1 And it reduces blue light significantly to lessen eye strain.

*The Dynamic AMOLED display on Galaxy Note20 Ultra received a certification from VDE Germany for 100 percent Mobile Color Volume in the DCI-P3 color range, which means your images aren’t washed out and you’ll get unbelievably vivid colors regardless of differing levels of brightness. The display can achieve peak brightness of up to 1500 nits, improving the contrast between dark and light aspects of digital content for a more brilliant picture quality, with a 3,000,000:1 contrast ratio to make your mobile experience more immersive.

*SGS, the world's leading certification company, awarded Galaxy Note20 Ultra's display the Eye Care Certification based on its ability to drastically reduce the harmful effects of blue light. This certification can be found on www.sgs.com.

ADAPTIVE 120HZ DISPLAY

Double the refresh rate for an incredibly smooth view

The 120Hz display makes scrolling silky smooth and touch interactions incredibly responsive.2 That also makes writing with the S Pen feel like writing with a real pen.3 It even intelligently detects what you’re doing and automatically shifts the refresh rate, saving on battery life and giving you more time for fun.

GAMING

The phone made to game

An epic gaming session can happen anywhere — and it's on a whole new level with a newly renovated intelligent processor and the smooth 120Hz display.2 Add in Wi-Fi 6 and Wi-Fi Optimizer to keep you connected fast with low latency, and you can game on in real time.4

The fastest processor in Galaxy

The upgraded processor packs more performance into its chipset, enabling you to take advantage of fast downloads and cloud gaming — without buffering, stuttering, or a surprise appearance by that annoying loading wheel.4

Make deleting a choice

STORAGE

Store thousands of high resolution photos and videos6

With the power of S Pen and 8K video technology, your creativity knows no bounds — including storage. Galaxy Note20 and Note20 Ultra can hold over 1TB total with up to 256GB built-in and up to 1TB expandable memory via microSD card.5, 7

Power to keep you working and playing all day, worry free

ALL-DAY INTELLIGENT BATTERY

Power to outlast your day

Maxing out at a whopping 4500mAh (typical) battery capacity, Galaxy's most intelligent battery makes power use more efficient to last all day.8, 9 So it gets you through the workday, and keeps going way into your late-night fun.

VIDEO CALLING

Say "Hello" to better video calling

Google Duo delivers Full HD video calling that works across devices to make sure you never miss a moment with the people who matter most. Wi-Fi 6 and Wi-Fi Optimizer keep the connection fast, secure, and with super low latency, so your calls stay clear.4, 14

Two large screens, delivering way more power

SAMSUNG DEX

A second screen experience that doubles the possibilities

Samsung DeX allows you to wirelessly link up to your TV and watch a movie or multitask with several apps via DeX while you catch up with a friend on your phone.15, 16 You can even turn Galaxy Note20 and Note20 Ultra into a touchpad or use Bixby on your phone for powerful and convenient control.

*At launch only TV will be supported. Monitor support is scheduled to roll out in October.

Sharing made easy and to the point

EASY SHARING

Point to share — it's that simple

Sharing files is simple and faster than before with the addition of advanced spatial awareness technology to Galaxy Note20 Ultra. Simply pull up the sharing panel and point your phone at your friend's phone to quickly send off photos and videos.17
