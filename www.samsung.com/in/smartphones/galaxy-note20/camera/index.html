8K VIDEO RECORDING

Capture your world in the highest resolution on a smartphone

Four times higher resolution than 4K UHD means way more detail and crisp recording — and now you can deliver pro-grade 8K video with director-level control. Take full control of the shot with ISO, shutter speed, manual focus, and more options in Pro Video mode. You can also shoot in 24fps and choose between 16:9 or 21:9 aspect ratios to enhance the cinematic feeling.

Play

PRECISION CONTROL

Play director with the tap of a finger

Pro mode gives you incredible control over your 8K recording. Control the zoom speed — zooming in slowly for a dramatic closeup, or zooming out fast for a grand reveal of the whole scene.

*Image simulated for illustration purposes. Actual UI may be different.

MICROPHONE CONTROL

Switch up the audio to level up your production quality

Connect your phone to a Bluetooth microphone — like any Galaxy earbuds — to have full control over audio volume and isolate your subject's voice.1 Or choose the microphone you want to use on Galaxy Note20 and Note20 Ultra, switching from front to rear or using both. So you get more of the sounds you want, and less of what you don’t.

*Image simulated for illustration purposes. Actual UI may be different.

*When play button is clicked, sound will automatically begin playing.

120FPS FHD RECORDING

Action as real as when you shot it

Set the camera to 120fps to shoot super smooth, detailed video. Keep it in real time or slow it down for slow-motion videos with less warping, less shudder, and way more action. Then when you play it back, the display automatically shifts to 120Hz for the full effect.2

LIVE FOCUS VIDEO

Add depth and flair to your videos

Adjust the background depth for DSLR-like focus in your vlogs. Then heighten the magic of the scene choosing from a variety of effects and shifting their intensity: Blur, Big Circle, Color Point, and Glitch.

8K VIDEO SNAP

Turn every 8K frame into a 33MP snapshot

8K means a seriously high resolution video — so every frame is high resolution too. Drag the timeline to find the perfect moment that you might have missed before.

Revolutionary camera AI for shareworthy shots

SINGLE TAKE

Advanced AI turns one shot into multiple formats

Tap the shutter and record for a few seconds (you choose the time), then let your phone do the post-production work. It delivers several different formats and even suggests the best one. And for portraits, it gives you studio-level photos with a pop of color in the background.3

*Image simulated for illustration purposes. Actual UI may be different.

Play

SUPER STEADY

Leave shaky video in the dust

Epic adventures deserve to be shared, and with Super steady on Galaxy Note20 and Note20 Ultra, the action footage you shoot comes out steady and clear. Advanced AI stabilization smooths out the shot, and bigger pixels keep movement crisp.

Play

More pixels, more zoom, more light

ULTRA HIGH RESOLUTION

The crispest and clearest photos in any galaxy

Millions more pixels means exponentially more detailed photos. With 108MP on Galaxy Note20 Ultra and 64MP on Galaxy Note20, you can pinch in on the background and see it just as clearly as the whole picture.

*Image simulated for illustration purposes. Actual UI may be different.

A 108MP capture taken on Galaxy Note20 Ultra of a woman standing in front of a wall of plants. You can zoom in and see the details of the photo clearly.

Pinch in to experience 108MP

SPACE ZOOM

Zoom in. More. More. 50x more

Get closer to the moment with 3x Hybrid Optic Zoom on Galaxy Note20 or the 5x Optical Zoom on Galaxy Note20 Ultra.4 Then add in Super Resolution Zoom enhanced by AI to zoom in 30x or 50x more respectively — without losing any details.5

*Image simulated for illustration purposes. Actual UI may be different.

Two women sitting on a beach. As you zoom in, you see a man doing something in the background. As you zoom in further, you see that he is building a massive sand castle.

NIGHT MODE

The biggest leap in low light photo in a Galaxy

Enlarged pixel size and intelligent nonabinning give Galaxy Note20 and Note20 Ultra the power to shoot incredible color and crisp detailed photos when the lights are low.6 Switch to Night mode and it instantly turns multiple frames into one well-lit, clear snap.

The three powerful cameras that keep you honing your craft

Go wide with the 12MP Ultra Wide Camera. Brighten up low light shots with the 108MP Wide-angle Camera, or get even closer with the 5x Optical Zoom on the 12MP Telephoto Camera.
